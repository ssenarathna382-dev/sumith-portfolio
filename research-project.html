AI-Based Prediction of Pile-Induced Construction Noise

Machine Learning-Based Noise Prediction and Screening Framework

Research Overview

This research focuses on predicting construction noise generated during pile construction activities using field measurements, activity information, soil parameters, distance, elapsed time, and machine learning models.

The study aims to support construction noise assessment, environmental risk identification, buffer zone planning, and smart construction decision-making.

Tools: Python, Machine Learning, Flask, Google Colab, VS Code, Data Visualization

Research Areas: Construction Noise, Environmental Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Smart Construction

Future Direction: BIM-integrated digital twin platform for construction environmental risk prediction.
